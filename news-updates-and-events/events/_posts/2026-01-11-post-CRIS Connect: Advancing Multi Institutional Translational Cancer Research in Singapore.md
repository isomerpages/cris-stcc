<p>As the Platform Lead for the Singapore Translational Cancer Consortium
(STCC) Translational Research Integration &amp; Support (TRIS) platform,
Asst Prof Jason Chan leads various multi-institutional projects at the
national-level which focus on developing, testing, and validating potential
new biomarkers and molecular diagnostics.</p>
<p>Asst Prof Jason is also a senior consultant medical oncologist at Division
of Medical Oncology, National Cancer Centre Singapore (NCCS). He concurrently
serves as the Director at NCCS Cancer Discovery Hub and Division of Medical
Oncology Research.</p>
<p>Asst Prof Jason has been actively involved in research and education.
While Asst Jasonâ€™s special clinical interests are in lymphomas and rare
cancers, his key research interests lie in unravelling the causes and molecular
pathobiology of rare cancers with the aim of improving clinical care of
patients inflicted with these diseases.</p>
<p>He is a Principal Investigator (PI) of the STARLIGHT Initiative for Rare
Cancer Research and Education, theme PI for SYMPHONY (Singapore Lymphoma
Translational Study) and Executive Committee member for TETRAD (TargEted
TheRApy for blooD Cancer).</p>
<p>He was awarded the Singapore National Medical Research Council (NMRC)
Transition Award in 2021. For his mentorship efforts of medical students,
clinical residents and budding researchers, Asst Prof Jason has been conferred
the National University of Singapore Dean's Award for Teaching Excellence
and SingHealth RiSE Outstanding Faculty Awards.</p>
