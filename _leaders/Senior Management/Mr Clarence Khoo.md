<p><strong>Mr Clarence Khoo</strong> is the Director of Operations and Strategy
with the Singapore Translational Cancer Consortium. A clinical pharmacist
by training, he is experienced in clinical development, project management
and regulatory affairs, having worked in multinational pharmaceutical companies,
hospitals and the Singapore regulatory agency. In the roles in the pharmaceutical
industry, he has had responsibilities for the planning and operationalisation
of regional as well as global clinical studies (early phase to late phase
development programmes and non-interventional studies).</p>
